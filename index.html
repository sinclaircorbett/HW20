Sara Sanctuary website was designed to allow visitors to build trust with the organization and donate to the cause. We have observed that Sara’s website donation process does not flow smoothly and does not build trust with the visitors, which is causing many visitors to leave the site and not donate to the non-profit. How might we improve Sara’s website so that we increase the number of donations?

STAKEHOLDERS INTERVIEW PLAN

Objectives:

-To find out what Sara Sanctuary’s branding is.

-To understand Sara Sanctuary’s current audience and who they want for the future.

-To find out their main priorities and what they would like to change.

Insights:

-6000+ visitors a month, some visitors were so determined to donate that they would email bc process was complicated.

-They wanted the tone to be positive, but impactful.

-Their main priorities are donations, volunteers, and adoptions.

USER TEST INSIGHTS

-During our interviews, we discovered that the majority of interviewees felt uninformed and unsecure about the donation process.

-Therefore, we believe that by creating a more clear, trustworthy donation page and web-presence might increase the amount of donations and user return to the website.

-We might do this by redesigning the information architecture, the hierarchy, the navigation system, and making the donation information more clear to our visitors. Doing this will allow our website to be trustworthy to donate and increase traffic of visitors.
